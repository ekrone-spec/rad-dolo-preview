Questions, answered

Before you ask.

Where do you offer personal styling?

In person across Minneapolis, St. Paul and the Twin Cities metro — and virtually anywhere in the world over video call.

How does virtual styling work?

You hop on a video call and walk me through your closet and your goals. I build a personalized plan — looks, shopping lists and outfit formulas — that works for your body and your budget, wherever you live.

How do I start?

With a free call. You dish about your style and goals; I tell you exactly how I can help. Then I build your plan and we start creating looks.

Do I need a big budget?

No. Every plan is built around your budget — and often starts with a closet edit of pieces you already own. Your disheveled closet is my happy place.

What services do you offer?

Closet edits, wardrobe revamps, personal shopping, event styling, photoshoot styling, vacation styling and family styling.

Questions, answered

Before you ask.

Where do you offer styling?

In person across Minneapolis, St. Paul and the Twin Cities metro — and virtually anywhere over video call.

How does virtual styling work?

You hop on a video call and walk me through your closet and goals. I build a personalized plan — looks, shopping lists and outfit formulas — that works for your body and your budget, wherever you live.

How do I start?

With a free call. You dish about your style and goals; I tell you exactly how I can help. Then I build your plan and we start creating looks.

Do I need a big budget?

No. Every plan is built around your budget — and often starts with a closet edit of pieces you already own.
